Industrial Metal Printing

DMLS / SLM Process

Direct Metal Laser Sintering and Selective Laser Melting for geometries impossible with traditional machining — internal channels, lattices, and undercuts at no extra cost.

02

Heat Treatment

HIP densification at 2,000°F + 15,000 psi. Precipitation hardening brings 17-4 PH to 50-52 HRC — matching forged tool steel properties for demanding tooling.

CNC Finishing

Post-print CNC machining to ±0.001" tolerances. CMM dimensional verification standard for aerospace and medical applications requiring tight specifications.
